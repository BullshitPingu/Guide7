Guide7

App of the student council of department 07 of the Munich University of Applied Sciences

Notice board

Stay always up-to-date with notifications when new entries are added. Use the search function to filter the list to your need in no time!

Week plan

Get your personal week plan from the ZPA. Additionally you may create your own custom events for events outside your ZPA schedule. Get notifications when ever a plan change is recognized.

Room search

Ever wandered around and searched for a free room? Just enter the date and time you need a room and BOOM!

Appointments

Never lose track of important appointments as you'll receive a notification a day before!

Interested? Take a look in our GitHub repository or download the App from the Google Play Store
